How to get here

by Bus

From Bangkok (Northern Bus Terminal), Chiang Mai you can take direct bus to Nan (but you must , because Rong Kwang is betwen Phrae city (30 km away) and Nan city (100 km away).
Also you can travel from Luang Prabang (at 9 AM) to Nan and than you can take bus (at 19:00) to Rong Kwang.

12go.asia - Online booking transport tickets

by Train

By train you can travel to Den Chai (from Bangkok or Chiang Mai) and then, by bus you can reach to Phrae, Rong Lwang.

12go.asia - Online booking transport tickets

be Plane

Phrae city have an airport, so you can come to Phrae city. Then take local transport (such as Song Thaeo taxi, Tuk-Tuk or motorbike taxi) to Rong Kwang. It can be found near.
